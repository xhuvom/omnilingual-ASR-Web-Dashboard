# CITY BANK CUSTOMER KNOWLEDGE BASE

## 1. General Information
- **Bank Name:** City Bank (সিটি ব্যাংক).
- **Hotline:** 16234 (One-Six-Two-Three-Four).
- **Website:** www.citybank.com.bd.
- **Head Office:** City Bank Center, Gulshan Avenue, Dhaka-1212.

## 2. Account Services
- **Savings Account:**
  - Minimum Opening Deposit: BDT 2,000 (দুই হাজার টাকা).
  - Interest Rate: 4.5% (চার দশমিক পাঁচ শতাংশ) per annum.
  - Requirements: NID Copy, 2 Passport Size Photos, Nominee Photo.
- **Student Account:**
  - No monthly fees.
  - Daily Withdrawal Limit: BDT 20,000 (বিশ হাজার টাকা).

## 3. Card Services
- **Credit Card limit:** Determines on salary range.
- **Card Blocking:**
  - If a card is lost, call 16234 immediately.
  - Press 1 for Lost Card reporting.
- **Replacement Fee:** BDT 500 (পাঁচশ টাকা) + 15% VAT.

## 4. Digital Banking (City Touch)
- **Login Issues:** User must reset password via "Forgot Password" link or call helpline.
- **Transaction Limit:**
  - Daily: BDT 5,00,000 (পাঁচ লক্ষ টাকা).
  - Per Transaction: BDT 1,00,000 (এক লক্ষ টাকা).

## 5. Loan Information
- **Personal Loan:**
  - Interest Rate: 9% (নয় শতাংশ).
  - Processing Fee: 0.5% (শূন্য দশমিক পাঁচ শতাংশ).
  - Maximum Tenure: 60 months (ষাট মাস).
